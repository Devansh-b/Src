Events

Chemical Engineering Jeopardy is an educational game that adapts the format of the TV show "Jeopardy!" to the field of chemical engineering. Participants answer questions in the form of questions, testing their knowledge of chemical engineering concepts in a fun and competitive way. It's a versatile tool for learning and engagement in the field.

AIChE Poster Presentation: Join us for an exciting showcase of cutting-edge research and innovations in chemical engineering at the AIChE Annual Meeting. Explore a diverse range of topics, network with industry leaders, and engage in thought-provoking discussions. Don't miss this opportunity to discover the future of chemical engineering. See you at AIChE!

Chem-E-Car at AIChE: Experience the thrill of innovation and competition at the AIChE Chem-E-Car event. Witness teams of aspiring chemical engineers design and build small, chemically powered cars to compete in a race. This exciting showcase combines engineering ingenuity and creativity. Join us at AIChE and witness the future of sustainable transportation!

AIChE K-12 Competition: Inspiring young minds in the field of chemical engineering! Join us at AIChE's K-12 competition, where students showcase their scientific prowess through creative projects and experiments. Witness the next generation of innovators and scientists as they tackle real-world challenges. It's a celebration of curiosity, learning, and STEM excellence!

Chem-E-Cube at AIChE: Step into a world of chemical engineering innovation and discovery! At AIChE's Chem-E-Cube, you'll encounter a dynamic, interactive space filled with engaging exhibits, educational activities, and fascinating experiments. Dive into the exciting realm of chemistry and engineering, where you can learn, explore, and be inspired. Don't miss this opportunity to experience the future of chemical engineering up close!

AIChE Cultural Night: Celebrate diversity and unity at AIChE's Cultural Night. Join us for a captivating evening of global cultures, featuring music, dance, cuisine, and art from around the world. It's a unique opportunity to connect, share, and appreciate the richness of our global engineering community. Come together and experience the beauty of cultural exchange at AIChE.
